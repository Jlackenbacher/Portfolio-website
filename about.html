ABOUT ME

My Story

A passionate Designer with reative direction & implementation experience successfully leading teams of designers, freelancers, and vendors in the art of problem-solving and collaboration. Continuously demonstrate strong leadership skills in a creative, cross-functional, and multidisciplinary environment, providing innovation and solutions for print, digital, and UI/UX design.

Born and race in beautiful Colombia (with “o” vs. “u”) in South America. I graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in Graphic Design when HTML started to become a common word. After running my own business for a year, I decided it was time to learn a new language and travel the world, as I loved and still love traveling. So I went to Vancouver, Canada, to learn English as a second language and stayed for another year and a half studying New Media. It is what we called today HTML, animation, video, and web.

During my time in Canada, I reconnected with an old friend. He became my husband after two years of a long-distance relationship. After that, I moved to San Diego, CA, where I live today, with two amazing daughters and 2 beautiful dogs.

My passions are: my family, traveling, animals —except for reptiles—, listen to a great novel and biographies (on my Audible), OTF, and Design Conferences.

I grew from a designer to a manager. I executed visual solutions for all internal and external publications in a wide range of projects, simultaneously innovating and improving project management systems. I want to bring what I have learned in the past to my new jobs while always trying to learn new things from work and my new colleagues.
